Green Turtle

Chelonia Mydas

Information

Green turtles, with a scientific name of Chelonia Mydas are known to be one most the most popular marine turtle species. They tend to live in open waters or near coastal areas and beaches. They have a length of 31-47 inches and weigh 150-400 pounds. Unfortunately, green turtles are known to be endangered due to their eggs being harvested for human consumption.
